Magic Cake

Magic cake is one of the tastiest cakes the world has ever known.Few have tried it and loved to tell about it and these are their stories.We also need to stress that whatever happens toyou as a result of consuming Magic Cake is your responsibility.You've been warned and you can view it in our legalpage for more information.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs,sugar,butter in a small bowl.
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
  4. Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
